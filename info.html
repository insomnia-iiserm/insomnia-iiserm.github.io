SURVIVAL GUIDE

PROTOCOL FOR ATTENDEES

COORDINATES

IISER Mohali, Knowledge City, Sector 81, SAS Nagar, Manauli PO, Punjab 140306.


HOW TO REACH

  • By Air: Chandigarh International Airport (IXC) is 10km away.
  • By Train: Mohali Railway Station (SAS Nagar) is the nearest. Chandigarh Station is 15km away.
  • By Road: We are located on the Airport Road. Use Uber/Ola to "IISER Mohali Gate 1".

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is entry free?
A: Yes, entry is free for all college students with a valid ID card.


Q: Can I bring a camera?
A: DSLRs are allowed, but drones are strictly prohibited without prior permission.


Q: Is accommodation provided?
A: Accommodation is available for registered participants only. Contact the hospitality team.

CODE OF CONDUCT

  • Valid College ID is mandatory for entry.
  • Alcohol, drugs, and weapons are strictly prohibited. Zero tolerance policy.
  • Any form of harassment or misconduct will lead to immediate expulsion.
  • Respect the campus property and green zones.
